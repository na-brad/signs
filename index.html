SIGN INFORMATION

Signage is extremely important to any successful business. A sign can serve many purposes, such as mark the location of your business, brand a location or even influence purchasing decisions. If you are new to signs, please familiarize yourself with the following sign information.

Channel Letter Sign

A channel letter sign is a sign composed of a series of individual Illuminated letters or graphics. These signs are found everywhere and are usually used to illuminate storefronts. From shopping malls, to exterior storefront identification, channel letters are the best way to see your business name in lights.

Box Sign

A box sign is any series of illuminated signs housed in a box that is either wall mounted or positioned on a pole. The box sign is the most popular and effective cost sensitive sign for any small business. Box signs are constructed to any size to meet any applicable zoning codes and ordinances.

Neon Sign

Neon signs are illuminated tube signs that contain neon or other inert gases at low pressure. By applying a high voltage, the gas brightly glows a certain color, depending on which gas is contained in the tube. Neon signs are a popular night sign with their attractive lights and unique shapes. Popular applications of neon signs are open signs, bar signs, beer signs, and sports logo signs. Neon signs are also a popular choice for those looking for a creative custom sign because virtually every neon sign is custom fabricated by a skilled glass or tube bender.
